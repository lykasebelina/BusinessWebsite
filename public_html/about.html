Our Founding History

We came up with this business idea because all of us, as friends, share a love for clothes. Whenever we go out, shopping for clothes is always part of our activities. We enjoy collecting various clothing styles, and that passion inspired us to turn it into a business. One of our friends came up with the name “The Wardrobe Co.”

The business officially started on September 23, 1999, in Barangay Timbao, Biñan, Laguna. We specialize in offering a variety of clothing styles commonly worn by Filipinos. Our collections include casual attire, formal wear, and accessories that cater to the everyday needs of people. Over the years, we’ve built a loyal customer base, ranging from kids to adults, who travel just to shop with us. Our products are trusted for their high-quality fabrics and excellent craftsmanship.

In addition to selling ready-to-wear clothing, all the designs featured on our website are our original creations. Each piece reflects our creativity and passion for fashion, ensuring that every customer feels confident and stylish in what they wear.

When The Wardrobe Co. was founded, it was just a small shop in Biñan, catering to local customers. Through hard work and dedication, we gradually expanded and became a household name in the area. Our commitment to quality and innovation has allowed us to earn almost 1 million pesos annually, which is a testament to the trust and satisfaction of our customers. Today, The Wardrobe Co. stands as a symbol of our shared dream turned into reality, continuing to provide stylish, high-quality clothing for everyone.
